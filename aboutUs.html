Welcome at Mind Mendor

Our project addresses Mental Health Issues and the need to be aware about it. The project aims at helping people to get rid of their psychological problems by introspecting and understanding themselves through expert sessions, personalized counseling sessions, and curated blogs.

It also focuses on building a community where like-minded people can socialise, share their experiences and problems, understand things better and prosper. Additionally, people can also get information about different mental health problems, tips, solutions, etc.
